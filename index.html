Spirce

By Kyle Chang, Michael Chen, Pedro Covarrubias, and Tyler Trinh


Millions of people around the world bike daily, weather it be for convenience or for sheer enjoyment. Many people have the constant problem of running out of electricity on their phone or just have a straight lack of electricity.

One thing all these bikers have in common is how much energy they are wasting. So much energy could be produced while biking, it is a waste to not try and harvest it. This way people to bike to work or bike to a nearby village or market would be producing energy the whole way there. We decided to make it portable to make it more convient to bring around because currently, bike chargers are all attached to the ground. We used other products for inspiration and implemented multiple mechanics from different devices into our product.

We implemented our Computer Science Principles and Intro To Engineering and Design to create a model of our product and to create this website to advertise and explain what our product is about.
