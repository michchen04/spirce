Title of Project

Tens of millions of bikes are sold every year and there are tons of people who utilize this. Some bike to get to work or biking to deliver items is their work, while other bike for fun or recreationally. Bikes are a vital part of many people's lives and commonly use. So why don't we use bikes to benefit ourselves. Our idea is to create a decive you can attach to a bike to produce electricity. You can use the energy from the wheel turning to charge your lights or your phone. This idea has been made before but we intend to make it better by changing the material, placement, and design.
