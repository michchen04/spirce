Idea Creation



There were a few criterias we had to meet while brainstorming our innovation. We needed to fix an existing problem in the world, target a specific audience or group of people who needed this problem fixed, and we needed to make a new product or improve on an existing product.

We began our brainstorming by listing different possible groups that have fixable problems. These groups of people consisted of: Young Children, Teenagers, Young Adults, Adults, People in Developed Countries, People in Undeveloped Countries, and People with Disabilities

We then thought of different topics, or themes. These consisted of: Kinetic Energy, Thermal Energy, Electricity, Apps, Websites, Robotics, Security, Sound Waves, and Human Convience

We decided to use our own problems as inspiration and started thinking of our pet peeves, problems in our lives, and activites that we do. One activity that really struck us was biking. Biking not only applies to us but to almsot every single categories listed above. People of all ages and in all different places around the world bike. We thought, "How can we use the fact that tons of people bike to solve an existing problem?" We then looked through our topics and one seemed to really stand out, Electricity. That's when we decided to find a way to produce energy with a bike, maybe through an attachment. This could help bring more electricity to underdeveloped countries and can be convient to people in more developed countries, maybe to charge their phones or lights.

However, with a quick google search, this idea has already been made. However, there are a lot of flaws and is pricey. We know that we can make this attachment better and cheape. We are improving on an electricity producing attachment to a bike by changing the material, design and palcement.
